The 70% Governor

You are not designed to run at 100% capacity. Operating at maximum capacity leaves no room for the inevitable disruption of reality. The 30% buffer is mandatory for emotional spikes and cognitive drops. Use the slider to see the impact of overdriving.

The 72-Hour Recovery Ripple

After a major stress event, the system requires 3 days to recalibrate. Mistaking Day 1's adrenaline for "full recovery" leads to the error-correction loop. Observe the physiological energy trajectory.

Day 1: Adrenaline distortion. False energy.
Day 2: The Soup. Emotional instability. Raw.
Day 3: Exhaustion. The body finally lets go.

Minimum Viable Shift (MVS)

When tasks carry massive emotional weight, reduce the task to its smallest physical movement. Not "finish the project," but "open the file." Movement generates momentum.

🛡️

The Memory Shield

Working memory disappears in survival mode. Stop relying on it. Externalize everything. If it isn't written down or systemized outside the storm, it doesn't exist.

⏱️

The 10/20 Rule

Interrupt the body to interrupt the mind. 10 Minutes spiraling = change chemistry (cold water). 20 Minutes frozen = change environment (move rooms).

🚨 Action Protocols

During a crisis (Level 0) or when outreach is necessary but feels impossible, your higher-order thinking (The Pilot) goes offline. Do not attempt to improvise. Use these pre-built, strictly structured protocols and communication scripts to navigate back to safety.

⚠️ LEVEL 0: RESET PROTOCOL

  • 1.
    IDENTIFY THE COLOR Am I Red (Panicked), Purple (Spinning), or Blue (Numb)? Say it aloud.
  • 2.
    INTERRUPT THE BODY Splash ice-cold water on your face or step outside for 60 seconds of air.
  • 3.
    EXTERNAL BRAIN Stop thinking. Write the one thing causing the loudest noise on a scrap of paper.
  • 4.
    THE MVS (Minimum Move) Do one physical action that takes less than 10 seconds.
  • 5.
    HALT THE STORY Say out loud: "This is a chemical wave. It will pass in 90 seconds. I am safe."

💬 The Outreach Toolkit

Do not write during a crisis. Use these pre-loaded scripts to signal for help without the burden of explaining.

The Signal Flare

"Hi, I’m looking for support. I struggle with anxiety, overwhelm, and working memory. Because of this, I find it hard to explain things clearly under pressure. I may need a moment of patience or help with prompts to get the information out."

The "Blanking" Protocol

If the Pilot goes offline mid-conversation:

  • • "I’ve hit a bit of a mental block. Give me 10 seconds."
  • • "I’m having trouble finding the words right now."

Mid-Communication Reset

Physical Anchor: Press heels into floor.
Visual Anchor: Stare at one non-moving object.
The Phrase: "I'm just taking a breath to stay focused."
